Common Questions

We've Heard Every Concern — Here Are the Answers

How do I know my money is safe with you? +
Iri Owuro Travels & Tours is a CAC-registered business (RC 8260804) with two verified physical offices — one in Ikeja, Lagos and one in Abeokuta, Ogun State. We provide payment receipts, booking confirmations, and full documentation for every transaction. You can walk into our office before paying a single kobo.
What if my visa gets rejected? +
Our visa specialists prepare your documentation carefully to maximize approval chances. We review your documents, advise on the right approach, and follow up with embassies. While no agency can 100% guarantee a visa decision (that's the embassy's call), our track record is strong — and we'll guide you through any re-application process at no extra consultation charge.
How far in advance do I need to book? +
For best pricing and availability, we recommend booking at least 4–6 weeks in advance for international packages and 2–3 weeks for domestic or regional travel. Visa processing timelines vary by country — some take 5 business days, others 3–4 weeks. Contact us early and we'll advise on the ideal timeline for your destination.
Do you offer group or corporate discounts? +
Yes — we offer special group booking discounts for parties of 10 or more, and we have dedicated corporate packages for organizations. We've successfully coordinated trips for groups of 20+ and can handle large-scale travel management including staff retreats, company trips, and executive travel. Contact us with your group size for a custom quote.
What's included in your tour packages? +
Our packages typically include return flight tickets, visa assistance, hotel accommodation, daily breakfast, airport pickup & drop-off, and guided tours. The exact inclusions vary per destination — the Qatar Experience package, for example, also includes a Desert Safari and Inland Sea visit. We provide a full itemized list before any commitment, and custom packages can be built around your specific needs and budget.
